🏃‍♂️ Step Tracker – Your Healthy Lifestyle Assistant

Step Tracker is a simple yet powerful app that helps you track your daily activities. It doesn’t just count your steps — you can also view your past data with various graphs and boost your motivation.

📊 Key Features:

  • Real-time step tracking – Track your steps instantly while walking or running.
  • Access to historical data – Easily view your daily, weekly, and monthly past activity.
  • Variety of analyses – Examine your activity through graphs and statistics.
  • Calorie and distance calculation – See the calories burned and distance covered.
  • Set personal goals – Set your daily step target and achieve your milestones.

🎯 Who is it for?

  • Anyone looking to increase daily activity
  • Athletes and walking enthusiasts
  • Anyone aiming for a healthy lifestyle
